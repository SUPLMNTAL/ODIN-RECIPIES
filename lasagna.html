Lasagna

Description:

A treat love by humans and felines alike. Give yourself something delicious to look forward to on Monday night... or any night of the week for that matter

Ingredients:

  • 450g of sweet Italian sausage
  • 350g of lean ground beef
  • ½ cup minced onion
  • 2 cloves garlic, crushed
  • 1½ teaspoons of dried basil leaves
  • 1½ teaspoon of salt
  • 4 teaspoons of diced fresh parsley
  • 2 tablespoons of white sugar
  • 1.5 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 egg
  • 1 can of crushed tomatoes
  • 2 cans of canned tomato sauce
  • 2 cans of tomato paste
  • ½ cup of water
  • 1 teaspoon of Italian seasoning
  • ½ teaspoon of fennel seeds
  • ½ teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 12 lasagna noodles
  • 450g of ricotta cheese
  • 350g of sliced mozzarella cheese
  • 1 cup of grated parmesan cheese

Steps:

  1. Cook sausage, ground beef, onion, and garlic on stove top with medium heat until brown.
  2. Stir in all crushed tomatoes, tomato sauce, tomato paste, and water. Season with sugar, 2 tablespoons parsley, basil, 1 teaspoon salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and pepper. Cover dish and simmer for about 1.5 hours, stirring occasionally.
  3. Bring a large pot of lightly salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna noodles in boiling water for 8 to 10 minutes. Drain noodles, and rinse with cold water.
  4. In a mixing bowl, combine ricotta cheese with egg, remaining 2 tablespoons parsley, and 1/2 teaspoon salt.
  5. Preheat the oven to 190 degrees C.
  6. To assemble, spread 1 ½ cups of meat sauce in the bottom of a 9x13-inch baking dish. Arrange 6 noodles lengthwise over meat sauce, overlapping slightly. Spread with 1/2 of the ricotta cheese mixture. Top with 1/3 of the mozzarella cheese slices. Spoon 1 ½ cups meat sauce over mozzarella, and sprinkle with 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese.
  7. Repeat layers, and top with remaining mozzarella and Parmesan cheese. Cover with foil: to prevent sticking, either spray foil with cooking spray or make sure the foil does not touch the cheese.
  8. Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes. Allow to cool before eating.
