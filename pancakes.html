Pancakes

Description:

A great and delicious way to start any morning, pancakes are easy and quick to make. This recipe produces soft, fluffy pancakes you'll just love!

And the best part? Most of the ingredients are probably sitting in your kitchen! Get your apron on and lets get cooking!

Ingredients:

  • 1.5 cups of All-purpose flour
  • 1.5 tablespoons of Sugar
  • 1/4 teaspoon of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 1.5 cups of milk
  • 3 tablespoons of melted butter
  • 1 egg

Steps:

  1. Place the dry ingredients together into a mixing bowl.
  2. Make a well in the center to add the wet ingredients. Mix well.
  3. Pre-heat and lightly oil a hot griddle or pan. Scoop 1/4 cup of batter onto it.
  4. Cook for two to three minutes, then flip.
  5. Cook both sides until brown.
